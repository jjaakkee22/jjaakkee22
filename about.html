About Me

I’m Jasey Patterson and welcome to my about me. I grew up in a small town in North Carolina with two siblings. I’m a creative person at heart and love finding new, innovative ways to make things. I enjoy helping people and my goal is to leave the world a little bit better than I found it. With that core belief at heart, it helped to shape my views and the talents that I chose to focus on in life.

I love helping people solve problems and to just be there when someone needs me. With that approach on things is what lead me to my current job at Amazon. I am a learning ambassador in my department. I help new and current employees learn different types of jobs and roles that they can do inside the facility. Being in this role has given me so many opportunities to grow and develop as a person and bring my personal creativity to my workspace.

When I’m not at work helping people, I love to focus my time on my crafts. Since I was a kid, my mom taught me different things such as sewing, crocheting, cross-stitching, and many other creative outlets. I also love gardening, which pushed me to build my own greenhouse with the help of my dad. I enjoy growing a mixture of plants and vegetables. I often grow more then I need and offer the extras to my family and friends. I also help them start their own gardens when I can.

All of this has led to me currently pursuing an associate degree in Cybersecurity. It took a couple of trial and errors to help me decide on an appropriate path that best fit my character. That’s when I discovered the IT programs. With so many different paths to choose from and all the different things you could do with your degrees, I knew I was making the right choice for my career’s future!
